About

Dive into the delightful world of Sheepers, a free idle pet simulation game where you take on the charming challenge of raising and nurturing a vibrant flock of sheepers (similar to the creatures in your world known as simply, sheep). Feed and train your sheepers, then send them off to competitions to earn gold, allowing you to expand your farm and acquire even more colorful sheepers. Experiment with breeding to discover unique combinations, all while aiming to collect a sheep of each available color. The ultimate goal? Unveil the secret rainbow sheeper and achieve the pinnacle of your sheeper-raising journey. Sheepers is a blend of strategy, care, and discovery that will keep you engaged as you grow your own farm.

And as a bonus, all players who own Sea of Blood, will also have the opportunity to buy an exclusive sheeper egg from the shop!
