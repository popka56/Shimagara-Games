About

Sea of Blood is an Old-School Indie RPG where you traverse a diverse world filled with mystery. Explore the game world and discover new gear and crafting recipes to gain yourself an advantage in the battle against evil. Figure out the best paths to level up quickly and try your best at the punishing Hardcore mode. Sea of Blood does not hold your hand, and is not for the faint of heart.

New for the Steam release is the Survival Event. Find it and survive long enough to be rewarded with all-new powerful loot. Also, scratch that completionist itch by earning the new Steam Achievements!

Features

Play alone, or co-op with up to three other players.

Show your dedication with Hardcore Mode (Permadeath).

130+ quests to complete.

5 Dungeons to conquer.

4 World Bosses to defeat.

1 Survival Event to overcome.

Lots of Gear, Pets and Mounts to collect and craft.
